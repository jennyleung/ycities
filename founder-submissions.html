Founder Submissions

Be understood faster by the right investors. Get early feedback, increase investor interest, and map your path forward.

Home› Founder Submissions

Starting a venture is exciting — but breaking through early-stage uncertainty is hard. Risk is real, and its interpretation is inherently subjective. Two thoughtful investors can review the same company and reach very different conclusions.

The Pre-Diligence Lab supports faster, more intentional deal triage — without replacing investor judgment. When you apply, you submit your pitch deck for structured analysis by trained analysts. They synthesize your key assumptions, risks, and opportunities into Assumption & Evidence Briefs designed for active angel investors.

Why Apply?

  • Increase your odds of an intro meeting — your venture is presented with the terrain clearly mapped, making it easier for investors to prioritize.
  • Gain early, constructive feedback — refine your story with informed first impressions before investor conversations.
  • Reach a curated investor audience — your brief is distributed directly to active angels within the ecosystem.

Free to apply. Built to move you forward.
