About Mangrove Circle

Mangrove Members sustain the ecosystem where emerging and experienced innovators grow together. If you're an angel, alum, or an advocate for innovation across generations, consider becoming a Mangrove Member.

Your membership helps remove financial barriers for founders pursuing scalable impact — through a generous community of advocates and a platform designed to give back. As a Mangrove Circle member, your annual charitable contribution supports:

[Photo or illustration with mangrove/nature imagery — interconnected roots symbolizing the intergenerational community of learners and mentors]
🌿
Youth Programs

Funding scholarships and program fees so that financial barriers don't prevent motivated young people from accessing world-class entrepreneurship education.

🌿
Learner Stipends

Supporting participants who contribute their time and talent as analysts, mentors, and program leaders within the yCITIES ecosystem.

🌿
Digital Infrastructure

Maintaining and improving the tools and platforms behind our educational experiences — ensuring accessibility and quality for every participant.

Join to pay it forward — and become part of a community where every generation learns, inspires, and grows.
