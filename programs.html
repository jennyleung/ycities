Educational Programs

Building a shared language of judgment across founders, investors, and learners of all ages.

Home› Educational Programs

Like a mangrove ecosystem, our work connects investors and founders across ages and life journeys in a shared environment of learning and exchange. As both groups gain a clearer understanding of how judgment and risk operate in practice, the range of what gets funded begins to expand. The result is a ripple effect — more transformative solutions securing capital and more complex problems being addressed at scale.

A 2-Saturday virtual intensive exploring how real early-stage investment decisions are made through judgment under uncertainty. Discussion- and debate-focused — no lectures.
